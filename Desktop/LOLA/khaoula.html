A Letter for You

From Rayane, with everything I have

“
June 2025 — somewhere between the stars

My dearest Khaoula,

I tried to write this letter three times. The first attempt ended with me staring at a blank page for twenty minutes. The second ended with me accidentally closing the tab. This is the third — and I promise, this one stays.

You are, without question, the most beautiful and wonderfully stubborn person I have ever had the privilege of loving. You carry the weight of the world sometimes, and you do it quietly, like it's nothing — but I see you. I always see you.

On the days when everything feels too heavy, when the sky feels too low — I am here. Not in a poetic, metaphorical way. Literally here. On the phone, at the door, wherever you need me to be.

You make ordinary moments extraordinary just by being in them. You laugh and I forget what was wrong. You smile and I remember why everything is worth it.

You are not just the person I love — you are the person I choose, every single day, without hesitation, without conditions, without end.

Take care of yourself. Not for me — for you. You deserve warmth, rest, and a love that always shows up.

— Rayane
